**1. Executive Summary**

This 7-day campaign positions PlateOS as the essential ordering platform for Finnish restaurants that already handle their own delivery but are overpaying third-party platforms just for the digital ordering interface.

**Campaign Window:** February 26 -- March 4, 2026

**Primary Goal:** Generate 20--30 qualified leads (restaurant owners who book a demo or sign up)

**Secondary Goal:** Build brand awareness among Finnish restaurant owners

**Budget:** €300--500 total ad spend across Meta (Facebook + Instagram)

**Target:** Restaurant owners, managers, and food entrepreneurs in Finland

**Core Message:** You already have delivery. Stop paying 30% for just an ordering page. Own your platform.

**2. Why This Campaign Will Work RIGHT NOW**

Three converging factors create a once-in-a-decade opportunity:

- **Platform Exit:** A major delivery platform just ceased all operations in Finland after 10 years. Restaurants that relied on it are scrambling for alternatives.

- **Monopoly Risk:** The remaining dominant platform now has 70--80% market share with zero competitive pressure. Restaurants fear commission increases with no leverage to negotiate.

- **No New Entrants Yet:** The next major international platform hasn't launched in Finland yet. There's a window where restaurants are actively looking for solutions.

**3. Target Audience & Ad Targeting**

**3.1 Primary: Restaurant Owners & Managers**

- Age: 28--55

- Location: Finland (prioritize Helsinki, Tampere, Turku, Lahti, Oulu, Jyväskylä)

- Pain points: High commissions, no customer data ownership, paying 30% for a service they could own

- Key insight: They ALREADY have delivery --- they just need the ordering platform

**3.2 Meta Ads Targeting**

- **Interests:** Restaurant management, Food service, Ravintola, Small business owners, POS systems, Food delivery

- **Behaviors:** Business page admins, Small business owners

- **Job Titles:** Restaurant owner, Ravintoloitsija, Yrittäjä, Toimitusjohtaja

- **Location:** Finland, cities with 50K+ population

- **Lookalike:** Upload your 3 current client emails + restaurant contacts as custom audience seed
